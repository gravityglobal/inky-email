Intergrated Security Manufacturing (ISM), a leader in the design, development and manufacture of door entry and intercoms, has launched a new brochure to showcase its portfolio of IP-based solutions.

The range comprises a selection of outdoor panels and indoor monitors to target specifiers and M&E consultants considering door entry and access control products for new build developments and refurbishments of apartment buildings.

Read more

At Security Twenty 18 in Nottingham over 300 visitors gave a positive response to the new line up which gives ISM the capability to offer a solution to all of the local and commercial housing sectors.

Grant Jenkins who heads up ISM's Door entry division says he was delighted with the response at the show: "Visitors to the stand showed particular interest in not only the new IP Connect range, but also our existing intercom and door entry products, and how they can be integrated with the award-winning Genesys 2 ISMS."

ISM has appointed Roy Thomson as its new Business Development Manager to drive the new portfolio of IP-based door entry solutions.

"Although I am in the early stages of my time here, I have been encouraged by the reaction from potential customers to the new IP range," he says. "My goal is to raise ISM's profile in the IP market and ensure we are always part of the discussion when specifiers and M&E consultants are considering door entry and access control products."

Roy has had varied and broad experience working within the security industry across all major EMEA markets since starting as a young engineer. He has worked across an array of systems and sectors within security including Intelligent Access Control, Video Door Entry Systems, CCTV, Intruder Detection, Fire Detection, Biometrics, and PSIM. He also has experience of Asset Tracking, Building Management Systems, ANPR and Car Parking Systems.

Roy joins Grant Jenkins, Door Entry Manager and Aaron Plant, Business Development Manager. Grant says Roy is already proving to be a great addition to the team: "Roy has already identified opportunities among some of his existing contacts and developed tactical business solutions to meet their needs. Based on initial discussions, we are confident Roy and our new IP range will prove to be a great success."
